What Tacive delivers

A governed object your team can use in the next roadmap discussion.

The pilot is scoped around a judgment object, not a generic document handoff. Your team gets a defensible claim, the evidence trail behind it, and the conditions that would change it.

Judgment statement

A board-ready, falsifiable roadmap claim with a clear owner.

Evidence trail

Source anchors organized so your team can review why the judgment is defensible.

Boundary condition

Explicit limits on where the judgment applies and what it excludes.

Counter-signal

A named opposing signal that could change the roadmap call.

Version stamp

Last verified date, version, and update state.

Maintainer note

Owner note on evidence quality and the next update trigger.

You provide

  • Roadmap question

    One named uncertainty your team needs to defend, such as yield parity, module allocation, qualification timing, or supplier route credibility.

  • Company or supplier scope

    The target company, peer set, or WBG value-chain slice your team wants the judgment to cover.

  • Decision context

    How the judgment will be used: supplier screen, roadmap review, customer qualification read, or internal planning baseline.

Tacive returns

  • Defensible Judgment Object

    A statement, buyer implication, evidence trail, boundary condition, counter-signal, version stamp, and maintainer note.

  • Buyer handoff

    A compact walkthrough of why the judgment is defensible, where it is bounded, and what would change it.

  • Maintained pilot layer

    For T2, a focused set of comparable judgment nodes with review state, evidence depth, E/S band, and last verified date.

Not included

  • Unbounded company coverage

    The pilot does not attempt to cover every WBG company or every device category at once.

  • Ungated source dump

    Tacive does not send unreviewed source piles; the output is a governed judgment object.

  • Full financial outlook claims

    The pilot is scoped to roadmap judgment and buyer implication, not full-company financial forecasting.
